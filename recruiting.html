Product Designer

(Remote Contract-to-Hire)

We're seeking a Product Designer who can translate user needs into intuitive digital experiences. You'll work on products that have real impact, creating interfaces that make complex tasks feel simple. The ideal candidate understands how design shapes both product functionality and brand perception, creating experiences that users return to again and again.

The Role

  • Flexible contract position (part-time or full-time available)
  • 100% remote collaboration
  • Monthly rate: $10k-$15k based on experience and capacity
  • Potential path to permanent position for the right candidate

You Are

  • A designer who thinks about the whole user journey, not just isolated screens
  • Someone who can spot opportunities to improve how people interact with technology
  • Skilled at creating clean, intuitive interfaces for complex products
  • Naturally curious about how users actually behave in real-world scenarios
  • Experienced in creating digital products that make work more efficient
  • Someone who understands how design reinforces brand identity
  • Thoughtful about integrating AI in ways that enhance the user experience

Daily Focus

  • Create intuitive interfaces that simplify complex tasks
  • Design information architecture that helps users find what they need
  • Collaborate with developers to bring designs to life
  • Contribute to and maintain design systems
  • Ensure product design aligns with brand guidelines
  • Design AI-powered features that feel helpful and natural

Required Capabilities

  • Strong portfolio demonstrating thoughtful digital product design
  • Mastery of Figma and related design tools
  • Experience with information architecture and system design
  • Ability to articulate design decisions through clear rationale
  • Understanding of how design shapes behavior and enables capability
  • Comfort with ambiguity and evolving project requirements

Would Help if You Had

  • Experience with data visualization or complex information design
  • Understanding of front-end development considerations
  • Experience working in interdisciplinary teams
  • Interest in emerging technologies and their design implications
  • Experience designing AI-powered products or interfaces
  • Understanding of how brand systems and product experiences interconnect

We believe the best products grow from genuine understanding of how people and organizations actually work. If you approach design as a tool for revealing what's possible rather than just making things beautiful, we should talk.

Apply

If you are interested in a this position, please email letstalk@kei.inc and include a link to your portfolio or website. If you do not have a portfolio or website, please include some work samples. A resume is not mandatory, but helpful.

How to Connect

Email letstalk@kei.inc with:

  • Link to your portfolio
  • Brief note on what interests you about this role
  • Your availability and rate expectations
