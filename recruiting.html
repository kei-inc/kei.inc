Polymath Designer

(Remote Contract-to-Hire)

We're seeking a versatile designer who thrives at creating living systems in product, brand, and storytelling. Someone who sees design as both an art and a strategic tool—equally comfortable crafting high-fidelity UI and developing brand systems.

The Role

  • Initial three week contract with path to full-time
  • 100% remote collaboration
  • Weekly rate: $3k-$5k based on experience
  • Start date: November 12th

You Are

  • A systems thinker who naturally connects dots across disciplines
  • Equally energized by pixel-perfect execution and strategic problem-solving
  • Comfortable moving between product interface design, information design, and brand systems
  • Someone who thinks in frameworks but designs for humans
  • Mid to senior-level experience crafting digital products and brand experiences

Daily Focus

  • Design information rich interfaces and stand-alone data visualizations that balance beauty and function
  • Develop and extend brand design systems
  • Collaborate with strategists and clients across industries
  • Contribute to concept development and strategic direction

Required Capabilities

  • Mastery of Figma and Adobe Creative Cloud
  • Strong information design and typography skills
  • Experience with product interface design and design systems
  • Portfolio demonstrating versatility across digital products and brand design
  • Clear communication and strategic thinking
  • Ability to articulate design decisions and contribute to broader strategic discussions

Would Help if You Had

  • 100% remote collaboration

We value polymaths who can navigate complexity while maintaining clarity of purpose. If you see design as a tool for understanding and shaping systems, we should talk.
