The six most important things we’d like you to know about us and what we think.

01

Our name is Japanese for 'system' It’s a little more complicated, of course, but that’s essentially what it means. We chose the name Kei because we like to approach our work mindful of the bigger context and general connectedness of things.

Kei (系): A Japanese term denoting a system or interconnected series. In systems thinking, it represents the complex network of relationships and interactions within a whole.

02

It’s all connected. We look at problems through both microscopes and telescopes, taking into account the complex relationship between parts and wholes. Systems thinking helps us uncover hidden connections, tackle challenges from multiple angles, consider all stake-holders and potential impacts, consider the wider ecosystem, and create solutions that extend beyond each immediate challenge.

Systems Thinking: An approach that examines how parts of a whole interact, revealing hidden connections and broader impacts of decisions.

03

We’re a nose-to-tail operation. Kei bridges thinking and doing. We’re riddled with the battle scars that come from having spent years connecting tangible outcomes without falling apart in the process.

Founded Method. Dove off the Deepend. Built an Incubator. Started Magazines.

04

We’ve seen things. As strategists, designers, futurists, researchers, storytellers, technologists, engineers and entrepreneurs, we have years of experience of conjuring ideas from intuitions, and molding them into actual things that can make little dents in the universe. Our diverse expertise allows us to apply insights from one field to challenges in another.

Built TED's digital presence. Crafted Google's Material Design, the world's first comprehensive design system.

05

The future can be bright. This shouldn’t feel like a controversial statement. We often work for purpose-led organizations because like us, they want to create positive change in their industries and communities, and they believe problems have solutions. And in our experience those who think that solutions exist are more likely to find them.

Businesses driven by a mission to create value for society and stakeholders, not just shareholders. They aim to improve lives and communities while pursuing financial success.

06

Our impact outlasts us. We don't just deliver results; we cultivate capabilities. By working alongside our clients and transferring our expertise, we ensure the positive changes we create together persist long after our engagement ends. We don’t just make the thing for our clients, we make factories that help them make the thing.

We have built labs and design systems for startups and long established brands alike, ranging from Google to Macy's.
