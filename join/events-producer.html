Events Producer

REMOTE - CONTRACT

Kei is a full-service creative studio that specializes in orchestrating complex projects. We're looking for a freelance Events Producer to own logistics and execution for a slate of events across LA and NYC.

The position:

We're launching a premium brand in the US market with a heavy events component—industry gatherings, consumer activations, launch parties, and pop-ups at partner venues. We need someone who can take an event from concept to execution: managing vendors, timelines, budgets, and on-the-ground logistics.

You'll work directly with Kei's founders on strategy while owning the details that make events actually happen. This is a part-time role with variable intensity—some weeks are heavier than others depending on the calendar.

The offer:

  • Compensation: $3,000-4,000 per month (contractor rate)
  • Hours: ~20 hours/week average, variable based on event calendar
  • Duration: Through October 2026
  • Start: ASAP

What you will do:

Event Production (60%)

  • Own end-to-end logistics for events: venue coordination, vendor management, permitting, staffing, rentals, catering, A/V
  • Develop and manage event timelines, production schedules, and run-of-show documents
  • Source and manage vendors; negotiate contracts and manage budgets
  • Be on-site for key events to ensure smooth execution

Planning & Coordination (30%)

  • Translate event concepts into actionable production plans
  • Coordinate with internal team and client on event strategy and creative direction
  • Manage event budgets and track expenses against projections
  • Build and maintain relationships with venues and vendors in LA and NYC

Documentation & Process (10%)

  • Develop templates and processes for repeatable event formats
  • Document vendor contacts, venue specs, and lessons learned for future events
  • Contribute to post-event recaps and reporting

Who you are:

Required

  • 2-4 years producing events in hospitality, lifestyle, food & beverage, or related fields
  • Experience managing events in LA and/or NYC—you have (or can quickly build) vendor relationships in these markets
  • Strong logistics skills: you can manage timelines, budgets, and multiple vendors simultaneously
  • Organized and detail-oriented with systems that keep you on track
  • Comfortable working independently with weekly check-ins rather than daily oversight
  • Available for occasional travel to events and flexible hours around event dates

Strongly Preferred

  • LA-based (events are primarily in LA with some in NYC)
  • Experience with brand activations, launch events, or trade events
  • Familiarity with hospitality or food & beverage industry

Bonus Points

  • Experience with spirits, beverage, or bar industry events
  • Existing relationships with venues and vendors in target markets
  • You've produced events for brand launches, not just ongoing programs

How to connect:

  • Email recruiting@kei.inc with:
  • Resume or LinkedIn
  • Brief note (400 words max) on your relevant event experience and why this role interests you
  • 1-2 examples of events you've produced (photos, recaps, or brief descriptions are fine)
