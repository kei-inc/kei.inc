Social Media Manager

REMOTE - CONTRACT

Kei is seeking a Social Media Manager for a high-profile client project: launching a heritage craft spirits brand in the US market.

The opportunity:

Our client is a family-owned distillery. They're launching in the US market (LA and New York first), and working to introduce an entire spirits category to American drinkers.

The position:

This is a 6-month contract position with Kei with an option to extend beyond that. You'll work closely with the Kei strategy team and collaborate directly with our client's US leadership to establish their social presence and strategy.

We need someone who can help tell a compelling craft story, build community across bartenders and consumers, and help establish a spirits category in the US—all while making it feel discoverable and exciting.

The offer:

  • Compensation: $6,500-7,500 per month (contractor rate)
  • Duration: 6 months (December 2025 - May 2026)
  • Start: December 2025
  • Potential to extend contract

What you will do:

Content Strategy & Creation (60%)

  • Manage daily posting across Instagram, TikTok, and other platforms for both the main brand account and campaign-specific account
  • Create compelling content using provided photo/video library, design system, and your own event documentation
  • Develop and maintain content calendar that balances brand storytelling, product education, bartender spotlights, and consumer engagement
  • Document events and activations (product launches, industry events, bar takeovers) with photo/video for real-time and evergreen content

Community Management (25%)

  • Respond to comments, DMs, and mentions in brand voice across all platforms
  • Build relationships with bartenders, spirits writers, influencers, and engaged consumers
  • Monitor conversations about the spirits category and craft cocktails to identify opportunities
  • Flag insights about what's resonating, what questions people have, what's confusing

Strategic Contribution (15%)

  • Collaborate on ongoing strategy evolution in weekly check-ins with Kei: What's working? What's not? What should we try?
  • Inform content priorities based on community feedback and platform performance
  • Identify creator and influencer opportunities for partnerships or collaborations
  • Work with photographer/videographer on larger productions to ensure content serves social needs
  • Contribute to launch planning for key brand activations and industry events

Who you are:

Required

  • 2-4 years social media management experience, ideally in food/beverage, hospitality, or lifestyle brands
  • Portfolio demonstrating content creation skills: You can shoot, edit, and post compelling content yourself, not just manage vendors
  • Understanding of platform best practices: Instagram, TikTok, and emerging platforms—you know what performs where and why
  • Strong written communication: Can write in brand voice, craft compelling captions, adapt tone for different contexts
  • Self-directed and organized: Can manage your own calendar, priorities, and workflow with weekly check-ins rather than daily oversight
  • Scrappy and resourceful: Comfortable building from scratch with lean resources
  • Pacific time zone-based
  • Experience with paid social and influencer campaigns

Strongly Preferred

  • LA-based for access to photographing events in Los Angeles
  • Experience in food/beverage, hospitality, or alcohol industry
  • Understanding of bartender culture and on-premise dynamics
  • Familiarity with spirits, cocktails, or bar culture

Bonus Points

  • Experience launching new brands or categories (not just managing established ones)
  • Photography/videography skills beyond iPhone—comfortable with DSLR, lighting, basic editing
  • Experience in spirits education or bartender community engagement
  • Understanding of cocktail culture and bar trends

How to connect:

  • Email recruiting@kei.inc with:
  • Resume/LinkedIn
  • Portfolio or links to social accounts you've managed (please annotate which content you created vs. managed)
  • Brief cover note** (400 words max) answering:
    Why are you interested in this role specifically?
    What's your experience with spirits/alcohol brands, bartender community, or hospitality? (If limited, what's your plan for getting up to speed?)
