Halcyon

Illuminating the Path to Energy Transition

Introduction

The world is racing against time to reinvent its relationship with energy. As governments, corporations, and innovators grapple with this monumental shift, one stark reality emerges: making sense of the deluge of data is as crucial as the technology itself. Enter Halcyon, a startup with a bold mission to cut through the noise and accelerate the energy transition through AI-powered insights.

Nat Bullard, Halcyon's Chief Strategy Officer, frames the challenge with stark clarity:

Nat Bullard
Halcyon
Chief Strategy Officer

Globally, we need just under $200 trillion invested in the next three decades to reach net-zero emissions by mid-century. In 2023, we invested $1.8 trillion. Deploying that money is a capital markets challenge, but it is also a technical, business strategy, planning, and regulatory challenge.

The numbers underscore the magnitude of the task. The International Energy Agency reports global energy investment hit $2.4 trillion in 2022, with clean energy claiming over $1.7 trillion of that pie. It's a massive market, ripe for disruption – and that's precisely where Halcyon aims to make its mark.

Bruce Falck
Halcyon
CEO

Inception Story

Bruce Falck, Halcyon's founder and former head of Twitter's ad team, didn't set out to revolutionize energy intelligence. His journey from "building incredible machines to serve ads" to serving the planet began with a simple desire: to make a tangible impact on the world's most pressing challenge.

A conversation with Andrew Beebe at Obvious Ventures, known for its "world positive investing," set the wheels in motion. But it was Falck's deep dive into the market – over 100 customer interviews – that revealed the true opportunity.

Every single one of our customers is struggling to navigate the energy transition, They spend an inordinate amount of time understanding energy policy and regulation to move the world forward. We help them do that more efficiently.

This insight became the catalyst for Halcyon's mission: to build a data platform that could empower professionals to navigate the labyrinth of energy transition with unprecedented speed and clarity.

Business Design & Concept
The Challenge

The energy sector's complexity is legendary. Professionals wrestle daily with a Gordian knot of regulatory frameworks, market dynamics, and technological breakthroughs. Nat Bullard paints a vivid picture:"Today's information landscape is opaque, fragmented, noisy, and ever-expanding. That is a source of intense frustration for any analyst in a strategy or policy role. But with emerging artificial intelligence capabilities, it is also an opportunity."

The scale of this data deluge is staggering. IDC projects global data volume to hit 175 zettabytes by 2025, with energy and climate information forming a significant chunk of this digital tsunami. It's a challenge tailor-made for AI – but only if wielded with precision and purpose.

Falck puts Halcyon's value proposition in stark terms: "If we can help someone get to a good answer in two hours versus two days or two weeks, that's going to have a huge impact. The way I think about it is we're removing sand from the gears."

Kei's role in Halcyon's journey was fundamental from day one. We dove deep into the problem space, exploring concepts, crafting prototypes, conducting rigorous market analysis, and defining potential value propositions. Our team became an extension of Halcyon, shaping the company's direction from its very inception.

Reflecting on this phase, Falck notes;

It was company creation in the true sense.

From Vision to Reality: Securing the Future

With a clear vision in hand, the next hurdle was funding. Kei worked hand-in-hand with Halcyon to craft a compelling investor narrative, developing user personas, market analyses, and bespoke data visualizations that brought the concept to life.

The result? A $10 million seed round – no small feat in a landscape where, according to the World Economic Forum, 40% of energy industry executives cite regulatory uncertainty as a major investment barrier. Halcyon's promise to navigate these choppy waters clearly struck a chord with investors hungry for solutions.

Forging a Brand for the Climate Era

A revolutionary product demands an equally powerful brand. Kei led a comprehensive strategy process, interviewing founders, stakeholders, and investors to unearth the essence of Halcyon's mission.

"We kind of lacked ambition and imagination for what this thing could be," Falck admits. The work reshaped the team's perception of their mission and potential.

Our goal was to establish Halcyon as the trusted technology partner and leading information platform in the energy transition ecosystem while leaving latitude to potentially expand to additional market segments in the future. This strategy was crucial in positioning Halcyon to address a market that Bloomberg New Energy Finance projects will reach $2.1 trillion by 2030.

The outcome was a brand strategy that positioned Halcyon not just as a tool, but as a trusted partner in the energy transition journey:

AI with a Human Touch: Halcyon's Product Vision

At its core, Halcyon harnesses the power of large language models (LLMs) and generative AI to tackle the Herculean task of processing vast troves of unstructured data. But it's their use of retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) that sets them apart, grounding these models in domain-specific knowledge to ensure outputs are not just plausible, but genuinely insightful.

Bullard explains in a blog post, "LLMs allow us to ask questions and get responses in human language. They make our work happen in a way that it could not before, allowing us to do something with software that would have been extremely expensive or impossible to do only with humans."

The platform's key features reflect this blend of cutting-edge tech and deep industry understanding:

AI-Assisted Discovery

Purpose-built search infrastructure and know-ledge graph streamline research and analysis. An intuitive natural language interface makes it easy to interact with and interrogate data. Customizable filters, tags, operators, and outputs provide multiple pathways to relevant information.

Curated Data Catalog

Instantly accessible information aggregated from governments, utilities, corporations, and industry organizations. High-volume, rapid data ingestion from authoritative sources keeps users up-to-date. Transparent document library and query response citations include clickable sources to verify accuracy.

Collaboration Tools

Shareable and exportable workspaces foster collaboration and integrate into existing work-flows efficiently and securely.

Kei's challenge was to design an interface that made this complexity feel intuitive – a multi-modal system that could serve everyone from data scientists to policy makers. Our approach spanned the entire product development lifecycle, from customer journey mapping and feature conceptualization to high-fidelity prototypes and a comprehensive design system.

Charting the Course Ahead

Halcyon's journey from concept to execution offers a glimpse into the evolving landscape of AI-enhanced data technology in the energy sector. As sectors converge and professionals from diverse backgrounds pivot towards environmental solutions, Halcyon stands as a testament to the potential of cross-industry innovation.

The Halcyon project exemplifies Kei's model of engagement, where strategic design thinking can shape a company from its very inception, creating positive ripple effects that extend far beyond immediate project goals.

In the race against climate change, every efficiency gained can have outsized impacts. Halcyon's mission to make climate-related work more efficient and effective is a testament to the power of holistic, strategic design in shaping not just companies, but potentially the future of our planet.

Challenges remain in the complex and rapidly evolving energy sector, with regulatory landscapes shifting almost as quickly as the technology. The Inflation Reduction Act, which provides $369 billion in climate and clean energy investments, is just one example of the massive changes occurring in this space. Halcyon's success will depend not only on its technical prowess but also on its ability to navigate these choppy waters.

As Halcyon continues to grow, the foundation laid by Kei positions the company for long-term success. With a clear vision, strong brand, and powerful product offering, Halcyon is well-equipped to navigate the road ahead and make a significant impact in accelerating the energy transition.
