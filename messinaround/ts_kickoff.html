1. Overall Plan

Activation Approach

  • Brunch is our entry point — orienting activations around the mimosa/spritz occasion
  • Two distinct event types:
    • Launch Parties = media/influencer announcements
    • Activations = customer-facing venue collaborations
  • Phased approach: Spring pilot → Fall expansion
  • Total scope: 2 launch parties + 8 venue activations
1. Overall Plan

Target Venues

What We're Looking For

  • Youthful, social energy
  • Groups of friends, not families
  • Outdoor space is a plus
  • Strong social media presence
  • Known for drinks (the mimosa crowd)
  • Popular and busy

What We're Not

  • Craft cocktail bars
  • Formal brunch spots
  • Quiet neighborhood places
  • Family-oriented restaurants
