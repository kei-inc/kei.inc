Japan Production Details

4 days in May - naturalistic documentation

Locations & Subjects

  • Nikaido Distillery: Production process, stills, bottling, materials
  • LFS Ceramics: Kitchom bottle craftsmanship (very visual)
  • Izakaya: Cultural conviviality in authentic setting
  • Oita Surroundings: Sense of place, landscape, local culture

Production Approach

  • Style: Run-and-gun, naturalistic, documentary feel
  • Schedule: 3 core days + 1 buffer
  • Lighting: Natural light priority, minimal setup
  • Not fetishizing Japan - authentic cultural rooting
  • Video: "Moving stills" plus directed action moments
  • Paul's role: Second camera, creative direction, brand guardian

In Situ Production Details

2 days in LA - conviviality & product in context

Subjects

  • Day 1 - Social: Bar environments, gathering moments, atmosphere
  • Day 2 - Cocktails: Focused bartender craft and cocktail beauty
  • Product Placement: Back bar, in context
  • Action: Pouring, serving, enjoying

Approach

  • Talent: "Scrappy" - bartender friends, trade for drinks
  • Venues: 2-3 different LA bars
  • Crew: Photo assistant, video assistant, cocktail stylist
  • Video focus: Joy, ritual, conviviality in action
  • David's role: Brand consistency, art direction

Studio Production Details

2 days - controlled, composable assets

Subjects

  • Hero Bottles: All SKUs, multiple angles
  • Cocktails: Beauty shots, clean backgrounds
  • White Background: For layering/composition
  • Video Moves: Pans, orbits, zooms for editorial flexibility

Approach

What We're Creating

Photo Library

  • High-res images across all contexts
  • Multiple angles per subject
  • Raw + retouched finals
  • Organized by use case
  • Tagged for easy retrieval

Video Library

  • 15-30 second modular clips
  • Multiple aspect ratios (16:9, 9:16, 1:1)
  • B-roll archive for flexibility
  • Some finished pieces for immediate use

Asset Management: Complete library organized by category and use case, with usage rights documentation and brand guideline integration
