Kei’s AI Offer

Amplifying Human Intelligence

Artificial intelligence is reshaping our world and we're fascinated by its potential. But we're not interested in AI for AI's sake. We see it as a powerful tool to enhance human capabilities - a "bicycle for the mind," as one of our partners puts it.

Jakob Nielsen
Founder, UX Tigers
Former Principal, NN/g

Jakob Nielsen, a pioneer in user interface design, recently pointed out that AI is introducing the third major paradigm shift in how we interact with computers. We're moving from telling computers how to do things to telling them what we want to achieve. It's a subtle but profound change, and it's opening up exciting new possibilities.

Our Approach

AI that Works for People

We've found that AI challenges are as diverse as the organizations we work with. Some are just starting to explore AI, while others are pushing its boundaries. Here's how we typically help:

For Organizations New to AI

We start by looking at your existing workflows. Where could AI make things smoother or more efficient? We then design AI-enhanced processes that fit naturally into your organization. It's not about replacing people, but about giving them better tools.

For AI Innovators

If you're developing AI products, we can help make them more user-friendly and effective. We're not fans of the current trend of big, open-ended text boxes for AI interfaces. As one of our designers passionately argues, "I hate that. That's where we've gravitated towards from an interface design standpoint." We're exploring more intuitive, multi-modal ways for humans and AI to interact.

For AI Companies

We can help you stand out in a crowded field. This might involve creating a compelling brand strategy, designing an approachable visual identity, or developing communication approaches that build trust in your AI technologies.

For Businesses Needing AI Strategy

We'll work with you to develop a comprehensive AI roadmap that aligns with your organizational goals. This involves analyzing the competitive landscape and establishing frameworks for responsible AI development and deployment.

Our Core Capabilities

AI Strategy Development

We help organizations define their AI vision and align it with their broader goals. Our work with Vibrant Planet is a good example. We helped shape the vision for Land Tender, an AI-powered platform that's now managing 7 million acres of forest, contributing to global sustainability efforts.

User-Centric AI Design

We're always thinking about how to make AI interfaces more intuitive and engaging. It's not just about visual design - we consider all the ways humans communicate and interact. As one of our team leads notes, "There's plenty of ways that we communicate ideas beyond just talking. We gesture, we point, we look at things. There's all these other senses that are not currently part of the process."


Our work with Halcyon illustrates this approach. We tackled the challenge of making vast amounts of energy transition data accessible and actionable. The result was a multi-modal interface that allowed industry experts to quickly scan and understand complex information.

AI Product Innovation

We help refine AI product offerings, identify key features, and develop roadmaps. We keep an eye on emerging trends - for instance, IDC predicts that by 2024, half of our interactions with computers will involve AI-driven speech, text, or computer vision.

AI Integration and Adoption

Integrating AI into existing workflows can be tricky. It's not just a technical challenge - it often requires cultural and organizational changes too. We help organizations navigate this process, considering both the technological and human factors.

The Human Element in AI

Was excited as we are about AI's potential, we're even more interested in how it can enhance human capabilities. A recent Harvard Business Review article reported that teams of humans and AI collaborating outperform either humans or AI working independently by up to 40% in complex problem-solving tasks. That's the kind of potential we're eager to unlock.

We're particularly passionate about using AI to address global challenges like climate change and resource management. Our work with organizations like Vibrant Planet and Halcyon shows how AI can be a powerful tool for environmental stewardship and sustainable development.
