Lazaza

Crafting an Approachable AI Brand Identity

The Challenge

Lazaza, an AI-powered advertising platform, needed a brand identity that would make their advanced tech-nology feel approachable and empowering for small to medium-sized businesses. The challenge was to create a visual language that balanced sophistication with warmth, standing out in a tech-heavy market.

Our Approach

Brand Strategy

We explored concepts that emphasized "back to craft," "sophisticated with a little whimsy," and "friendly & approachable," aiming to counteract the often cold, robotic feel of AI branding.

Logo Development

We created a distinctive logotype that appears both hand-drawn and refined, carefully addressing readability issues, particularly with the letter 'Z'.

Color Palette

We selected warm reds and neutral tones to add vibrancy while keeping the brand grounded and accessible.

Typography

We chose typefaces that enhanced brand recognition and readability across various applications.

Lazaza’s new identity

The Challenge

The resulting brand identity successfully combines playfulness with professionalism, helping Lazaza stand out from competitors. The new visual language works effectively across various mediums and applications, providing Lazaza with a solid foundation for their brand communication. Current Engagement: Enhancing the User Experience Building on our initial work, Kei is now collaborating with Lazaza to refine the user interface and experience of their application. Our focus is on:

  • Streamlining the user journey to create a more intuitive, app-like experience.
  • Ensuring users always understand their current position in the process and what steps come next.
  • Aligning the interface design with the established brand identity.
  • Creating a 'magical and organic experience' that simplifies complex AI-driven processes.
