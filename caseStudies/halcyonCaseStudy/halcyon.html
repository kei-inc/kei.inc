Many people stop there, forgetting that every big whole is made from many small parts. Scaling has driven a lot of progress, but it’s also caused a lot of our problems. Research

Our primary interest was to understand the pain points and needs of various constituents involved in California land / forest / fire management to under-stand how specifically devised technology might help them. We created a far-reaching stakeholder engagement process to understand workflows, tools used, core needs, pain points, and imagined use cases.
