Kei is an independent strategic creative agency that helps organizations navigate complex challenges at the intersection of business, technology, and human experience. We bridge the gap between visionary thinking and exceptional implementation, enabling our clients to turn complex challenges into strategic advantages.

Our approach is rooted in

Systems Thinking

We alternate between microscope and telescope, understanding how individual parts interconnect to form a whole. This approach uncovers hidden connections, enabling us to craft solutions with broader impacts.

Strategic-to-Tactical Expertise

Our experience spans high-level strategy to detailed delivery of customer-facing touchpoints. This comprehensive perspective translates business intent into practical, effective outcomes.

Holistic Problem-Solving

We tackle challenges from multiple angles, considering the full constellation of stakeholders and potential impacts to develop thoughtful, integrated solutions.

Our name, inspired by the Japanese concept of "system," reflects our commit-ment to holistic, interconnected solutions. We believe that those who think solutions can be found are more likely to find them. As strategists, designers, futurists, researchers, storytellers, technologists, engineers and entrepreneurs, we've helped leading organizations grow, scale and amplify their impact.
